About it

"Seven Pounds" is a 2008 drama film that revolves around the main character, played by Will Smith, who plays the role of Tim Thomas, a man with a dark secret that drives him to seek forgiveness through acts of kindness and selflessness.

The film is composed of a sophisticated plot full of flashbacks, leaving the viewer with a confused idea about the time sequence of the scenes and the presence of the characters in the film.

This website offers a comprehensive and exhaustive perspective of the temporal evolution of the characters' importance within the plot, taking advantage of a line graph arranged distinctly within a jellyfish.
